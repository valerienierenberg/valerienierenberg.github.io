Rage Chess is a multiplayer Virtual Reality game that allows users to play chess and express their emotions throughout the game.

I worked with a development team of 4 people to build Rage Chess, and my primary role was Deployment and Documentation Lead.

  • Built For: Oculus Quest 2
  • Built With: Unity 2020.3.10, Photon Pun 2, Unity Collaborate, C#, GitHub, Visual Studio Code, Oculus Developer Hub, Oculus Quest 2
